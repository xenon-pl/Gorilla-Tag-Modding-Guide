# Locating your game

## Finding your game folder

In Steam, select "Gorilla Tag" from your app library.
Then, right-click on it or use the cogwheel, and select `Manage -> Browse Local Files`.

![visual guide](/assets//localfiles.png)

Most of the time, the default locations are:

- Linux: `~/.steam/steam/steamapps/common/Gorilla Tag`

- Windows: `C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Gorilla Tag`
